About Weather Wisdom

A hybrid weather forecasting system combining real-time predictions, statistical analysis, and climate patterns to deliver personalized weather intelligence for NASA Space Apps Challenge 2025.

Project Overview

Weather Wisdom is an advanced weather forecasting platform that addresses the challenge "Will It Rain On My Parade?" by providing personalized weather predictions across three time horizons. Our hybrid approach combines real-time meteorological data, historical statistical analysis, and climate patterns to offer users comprehensive weather intelligence for planning outdoor activities, travel, and events.


Unlike traditional weather services that provide generic forecasts, Weather Wisdom allows users to define their own comfort thresholds and query specific conditions like "very hot," "very wet," or "uncomfortable" days. This personalized approach empowers users to make informed decisions based on their individual preferences and needs.
